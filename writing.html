My Writing for the Web Experience

Overview of My Web Writing

With years of experience as a web writer and editor, I specialize in creating accessible, engaging content that resonates with audiences. My roles at Zima Magazine and Angliya Newspaper have helped me develop a strong foundation in online content creation and editorial management.

My Editorial Roles

As the editor of Zima Magazine’s website (2017–2019) and Angliya Newspaper (until 2017), I was responsible for managing content that aligned with best practices in digital media. This included crafting impactful headlines, editing for clarity, and ensuring all content was audience-focused.

Core Web Writing Skills

  • Headline Crafting: Writing concise, powerful headlines that grab attention
  • Clear Language: Simplifying complex topics into clear, accessible language
  • Structured Text: Using subheadings and bullet points for scannability
  • Inverted Pyramid: Prioritizing the most important information first
  • Readability: Keeping paragraphs short to maintain reader engagement
  • CTAs and Links: Incorporating strategic calls to action and links
  • Engaging Tone: Maintaining a friendly, relatable tone
  • Proofreading: Ensuring polished, error-free content
  • Multimedia Use: Incorporating images and videos as necessary
